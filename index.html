    About the Loafer’s Guide #

    This text was written by Carolyn Dougherty between 1987 and 1994, and then updated occasionally until 2004. When the Guide speaks in the first person, that’s Carolyn speaking.

    Some of the guide is naturally out of date — there is no longer a Library School in South Hall, and Boalt Law School is no longer called Boalt.
