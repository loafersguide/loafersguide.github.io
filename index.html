    How to read this guide #

    The navigation on the left links to the guide’s major sections.

    If a section has subsections, those will be navegable with the hyperlinks on the right. For a direct link to any heading on the site, use those right-hand-nav permalinks or the octothorpe by the heading on hover.

    The Search bar in the upper-lef-hand corner offers full-text search of the sections of the guide; for example, search for Maybeck.

    Addenda/annotations appear in color-coded sections.
